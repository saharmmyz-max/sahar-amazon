The Relentless Pursuit of Rare Books: Further Essays and Escapades Paperback - March 27, 2025

by Kurt W. Zimmerman (Author), 5.0 (3) See all formats and editions See all formats and editions

Author, book collector, and historian Kurt Zimmerman continues his entertaining and informative writings about relentlessly pursuing rare books. His first-hand escapades capture the contemporary world of rare books. His historical essays bring to life an incredible cast of rare book hunters including book collectors, antiquarian booksellers, rare book librarians, intrepid bibliographers, auctioneers, and even an occasional book thief or nefarious forger. The book is an irresistible read and significant contribution to biblio-history.
